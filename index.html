Play the game with basic rules
  • Choose your character, then your opponent
  • Attack until your opponent's hp drops to 0
  • Every time you attack, increase your damage by your base attack
  • Your opponent will deal a fixed amount of counter-attack damage back
  • Win by defeating all opponents, lose by running out of HP
Play the game with additional stats and skills
  • Choose your character, then your opponent
  • Take turns to attack each other
  • Gain additional stats and skills after winning each battle
  • Recover half your lost HP at the end of each battle
  • Udefeated opponent's stas and skill points go up after every battle
  • Win by defeating all opponents, lose by running out of HP
